## 🚀 Excited to Share My Latest Project: AI-Powered Personal Finance Manager! 💰📊

I'm thrilled to present my full-stack personal finance management application that leverages AI to transform how we understand our spending habits!

### 🎯 Key Features:

✅ **Smart Bank Statement Analysis**
Upload CSV statements from SBI, Axis Bank, or Kotak Mahindra - the system automatically detects your bank format!

✅ **AI-Powered Insights**
Google Gemini AI analyzes your spending patterns and provides personalized financial recommendations

✅ **Interactive Dashboards**
Beautiful visualizations showing spending by category, trends over time, and transaction patterns

✅ **Advanced Analytics**
Deep dive into your financial health with category-wise breakdowns, merchant analysis, and spending forecasts

✅ **AI Financial Advisor Chat**
Ask questions about your finances in natural language - get instant, contextual advice powered by AI

✅ **Mobile-First Design**
Fully responsive interface that works seamlessly on all devices

### 🛠️ Tech Stack:

**Frontend:**
• React 19 + Vite
• Chart.js for visualizations
• React Router for navigation
• Responsive CSS with professional banking theme

**Backend:**
• Python Flask REST API
• Pandas & NumPy for data analysis
• Google Gemini AI for intelligent insights
• Multi-bank CSV parser (SBI, Axis, Kotak)

**Deployment:**
• Frontend: Vercel (auto-deployment)
• Backend: Render (free tier with smart wake-up)

### 💡 What Makes It Special:

🔹 **Intelligent Bank Detection** - Automatically identifies CSV format and parses accordingly
🔹 **Real-time AI Chat** - Chat with your financial data like having a personal advisor
🔹 **Privacy First** - All data processing happens securely, no storage of sensitive information
🔹 **One-Click Demo** - Try it instantly with sample data
🔹 **Category Auto-Classification** - Smart categorization of expenses (Food, Transport, Investment, etc.)
